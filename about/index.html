OUR STORY

Built in Eldoret. Built for every Kenyan child.

WHY WE EXIST

The Kenyan school system is built for the average pace. One teacher, forty students, one speed. For the child who keeps up — it works. For the child who does not — the gap grows quietly until it becomes a crisis.

Ustadi Learning was built because that gap is not a reflection of a child's ability. It is a reflection of their environment. Change the environment — and the results change.

Every child we work with gets one tutor whose only job is to make sure that child understands — not just keeps up.

THE NAME

Ustadi is a Swahili word. It means mastery. Craftsmanship. The kind of skill that comes from genuine understanding — not memorisation.

That is what we are building — in every student we teach and in everything we do.

OUR STANDARD

Every Ustadi tutor goes through a structured assessment before meeting a student. Subject knowledge, clarity of explanation, and patience are all evaluated. We do not place anyone in front of a child until we are confident in them.

This standard is not negotiable.

Clarity over speed

We never rush a student to the next topic before they have genuinely understood the current one.

Honesty over comfort

If we are not the right fit for a student — we say so. And we recommend someone who is.

Access over exclusivity

Every first session is free. Because the best tutoring should be experienced before it is paid for.

THE FOUNDER

Ustadi Learning was founded by Ochieng — a designer, developer, and educator based in Eldoret, Kenya.

The belief that drives it is simple: every Kenyan student deserves to be taught by someone who knows how to make it land. That standard is non-negotiable here.
